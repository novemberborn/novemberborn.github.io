You could send me an email.

At State I help make a better internet around what people think. It’s pretty neat and you should sign up or even join the team.

I like Promises and so can you. Legendary is my implementation.

Building on top of Legendary there’s Streamist, which enables functional, promise-based interactions with Readable streams, and Aitch, a toolkit for constructing hypertext service clients. Thenstream can be used to construct a Readable stream from a thenable.

Other packages are chai-sentinels, which helps passing sentinel values through your code and asserting the results with Chai, and sixtyfour, a set of Base64 utilities.
